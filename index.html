About Me

I'm a Senior Visual Effects Artist and Colorist with two decades of experience delivering high-end finishing for national campaigns. My work with brands like Target, Best Buy, and Mayo Clinic has spanned thousands of projects across broadcast, digital, and streaming platforms.

What sets me apart is my ability to bridge traditional filmmaking expertise with emerging AI technologies. I don't just use tools—I apply decades of knowledge in composition, color, and storytelling to create polished, professional results.

From concepting and directing the music video "MRI" by The Symptones to leading complex VFX workflows for major agencies, I bring both creative vision and technical execution to every project.
